Top three activities to do at Klang

Famous Bak Kut Teh Restaurant

Klang is famous for its food and one popular dish is Bak Kut Teh ,a kind of meat bone soup with herbs and spices.

Visit The Galeri Diraja Sultan Abdul Aziz

It highlights the rich heritage of the Selangor Sultanate and contains an excellent collection of artefacts and gifts, together with replicas of the Selangor crown jewels.

Visit The Sri Sunderaraja Perumal Temple

This temple, thought to be 120 years old, is the largest Vaishnavite temple in Malaysia for devotees of Lord Vishnu. It is known as the ‘Thirupathi of South East Asia’.
